About Image Info Chatbot

Welcome to the Image Info Chatbot! This chatbot is designed to help you understand images better. Upload an image, and our AI-powered system will extract key details from it. You can also ask specific questions about the uploaded image, and our chatbot will try to answer them for you.

Our goal is to make image information accessible and interactive, whether you’re using this tool for educational, professional, or personal purposes.

Features

  • Upload an image and get a detailed caption
  • Ask follow-up questions about the image
  • Uses advanced AI and computer vision technologies
  • Easy-to-use interface for seamless interaction
